Welcome!

Hi, thanks for dropping by.

My teaching has focused mostly on ethics, and my research mostly on metaphysics. 

Currently I am a Visiting Scholar at Allegheny College and working mostly outside of academia.

Previously I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at Allegheny, an Assistant Professor at Boğaziçi University in Istanbul, Turkey, a Post-Doctoral Teaching Fellow at St. Louis University, and an Assistant Professor at Bilkent University in Ankara, Turkey.

I received my Ph.D in Philosophy from Syracuse University. I am writing a book on philosophy of fiction, especially on the concept of truth in fiction. I am also at work on a fantasy novel which, while about the notions of home and belonging, is primarily about violence, jokes, and the exploits of a team of D&D-playing Radio Shack employees from 1986 who end up on the wrong side of the multiverse.
